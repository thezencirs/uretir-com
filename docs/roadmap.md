# Roadmap

This is a living direction document, not a promise of delivery dates. Move work between horizons as evidence, capacity, and dependencies change. Each initiative needs a named owner, a measurable outcome, and an explicit decision to continue, revise, or stop.

## Now

- Establish the documentation operating system and keep it current with product changes.
- Establish the manufacturing knowledge graph: entity taxonomy, canonical aliases, typed relationships, source/review rules, and internal-link contract.
- Establish the editorial engine: source hierarchy, expert review workflow, freshness policy, and migration audit for high-value legacy pages.
- Stabilize the organic discovery platform: route quality, metadata, structured data, sitemap coverage, entity coverage, topic clusters, and publishing consistency.
- Define an analytics baseline for organic traffic, indexation, internal link quality, session depth, return behavior, topical authority, and entity coverage.
- Define product-specific data contracts and sample-to-live adapter requirements for PuanAI, TesvikAI, FiyatAI, and IhracatAI before connecting live providers.
- Define baseline performance and accessibility budgets for key templates.

## Next

- Introduce a durable content and entity source with explicit editorial workflow, relationship review, and revision history.
- Build the first Uretir ID backend slice only where it supports retention: sign-in, member profile, saved knowledge, privacy controls, and account lifecycle.
- Add follows and collections with durable data models after their discovery and return-value metrics are defined.
- Implement entity-page templates and internal-link modules that surface graph relationships without harming performance.
- Add observability for errors, performance, search health, and meaningful product events.

## Later

- Launch moderated comments and a verification program only after the required safety tooling exists and they can strengthen authority or useful contribution.
- Deliver personalized feed and notification preferences based on explicit follows and consented interests.
- Introduce UretirAI with sourced retrieval, evaluation, and conversation controls.
- Expand PuanAI and establish reusable opportunity-data patterns for TesvikAI and HibeAI.

## Future

- Professional company, expert, factory, tool, and product pages connected by a maintained knowledge graph.
- Multiple AI products sharing identity, preferences, governance, and trustworthy retrieval.
- Collaboration and contribution workflows for experts and organizations.
- International-ready architecture while preserving strong Turkish editorial depth.

## Vision

Uretir becomes the trusted operating layer where people can understand what is being made, who is making it, how and where it happens, why it matters, and what to do next.

## Roadmap governance

- Evaluate each roadmap item against the [sustainable growth strategy](./growth-strategy.md) before it enters Now.
- Review Now weekly, Next monthly, and Later/Future quarterly.
- Do not promote an item solely because it was planned; promote it when dependencies and evidence are ready.
- Record major reprioritizations in the decision log.
- Attach the primary growth outcome, success metric, and measurement window before implementation begins.
