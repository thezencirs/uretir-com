# Uretir SEO Constitution

> Permanent policy. If another document, metric, campaign, or deadline conflicts with this constitution, this constitution wins.

## Mission

Uretir exists to help people understand production. It does not exist to exploit search systems. When people receive a useful, accurate, understandable answer, search engines can recognize the value naturally.

Google is a distribution partner, not an opponent. Uretir earns recommendation by becoming one of the highest-quality, most trustworthy destinations Google can send people to.

## The user-benefit test

Before publishing a page, changing a template, adding a link, or training an AI workflow, ask:

> Does this genuinely help the user understand or decide something better?

If the answer is no, do not build or publish it.

## Non-negotiable prohibitions

Uretir never creates:

- Pages whose only purpose is to rank or inflate page count.
- AI spam, unreviewed AI output, or automatically generated filler.
- Keyword stuffing, doorway pages, deceptive redirects, or ranking manipulation.
- Duplicate content without a distinct editorial purpose and canonical strategy.
- Invented facts, statistics, company information, incentives, quotes, sources, or citations.
- Structured data that claims information not visibly and accurately supported by the page.
- Links added only to manipulate rankings rather than help the reader.

## Trust and evidence

Trust is Uretir's most valuable asset. State uncertainty when it exists. Identify official sources when they are used. Distinguish source-backed facts, editorial analysis, user claims, and AI synthesis.

Prefer official institutions, company announcements, public regulations, official reports, official documentation, and official statistics. When a primary source is unavailable, explain the limitation instead of presenting an inference as fact.

## Article integrity

Every article must answer a real question, solve a real problem, offer original value, use language people can understand, and be updated when relevant information changes. It must show publication date, last-updated date, source context, and a concise change log when material updates occur.

Reference-quality content is more important than publishing volume. Uretir would rather maintain 100 excellent articles than publish 10,000 weak ones.

## Topical authority, not keyword chasing

Uretir builds connected knowledge around durable topics, entities, and user questions. The five manufacturing pillars, topic clusters, entity relationships, and internal links are tools for comprehension and discovery—not mechanisms for manufacturing superficial pages.

## Human editorial accountability

AI can assist research organization, outlining, translation, classification, and drafting. It never replaces editorial judgment. A human owner remains accountable for accuracy, usefulness, sources, publication, and updates.

## Experience is part of quality

Fast, accessible, mobile-first, readable, and professional experiences are SEO commitments because they help real people. Performance, accessibility, and content clarity cannot be traded away for a growth experiment.

## Long-term horizon

Optimize for five years, not five days. Choose reusable knowledge, honest sources, durable URLs, maintained articles, and trustworthy habits over temporary traffic spikes.

## Enforcement

- The [SEO playbook](./seo-playbook.md) implements this constitution; it may add process but may not weaken a rule.
- The [editorial engine](./editorial-engine.md) turns the policy into publication and review requirements.
- A proposed exception must be documented in the decision log, reviewed by Product, Editorial, and Engineering, and must not violate any non-negotiable prohibition.
- Any contributor may block a publication or implementation that credibly violates this constitution until it is reviewed.

## Promise

Uretir never publishes content that exists only to attract clicks. It publishes because someone genuinely needs the answer. If a user leaves knowing more than when they arrived, Uretir has succeeded.
